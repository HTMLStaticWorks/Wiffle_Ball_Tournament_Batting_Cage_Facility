Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own bat?

We provide high-quality wiffle bats with all rentals, but you are welcome to bring your own as long as they meet our safety standards.

Is there an age limit for the batting cages?

Cages are open to all ages, however children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours in advance will receive a full refund. Same-day cancellations may be subject to a 50% fee.
